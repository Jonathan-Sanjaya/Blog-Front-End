THE LEGEND

UIAIUIIIAI

The sound of chaos. The face of judgment. The ultimate unit of floof.

Introducing UIAIUIIIAI, a cat so powerful their name can only be pronounced in pure confusion and awe. This glorious round unit spends most of the day loafing in places you least expect — the kitchen counter, the bathroom sink, or right in front of the TV during the season finale. With their wide-eyed stare, UIAIUIIIAI silently questions all your life choices while demanding snacks you didn’t even buy yet.

Fun Fact

● Nickname : The Orb, Loafzilla, UIAI for short
● Favorite snack : Literally anything edible (and some things that aren't)
● Hobby : Blocking doorways like a final boss
● Quirk : Sounds like a broken trumpet when meowing
“Once, UIAIUIIIAI sat in the hallway for 3 hours… no one dared to pass.”
